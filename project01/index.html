Respect & Dignity

Ontario Human Rights Code

Freedom from harassment and discrimination in employment and services, and fair and equitable access to all services

This is at the very heart of the TT's commitment to upholding all aspects of the Ontario Human Rights Code. The TTC serves the city of Toronto, which is a multi-racial, multi-cultural, and multi-linguistic city. The TTC recognizes and values the diversity, self-worth and human rights of its employees and customers by striving to treat each person with respect and dignity.
